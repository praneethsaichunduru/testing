Addressing an Article

The primary purpose of a properly written address is to ensure that the mail reaches its intended recipient without delays. A clear and complete address allows postal workers to quickly and accurately sort and deliver the mail.

When sending an article to a post office with

Levels of Postmaster

POST OFFICE ACRONYM/RANK POST OFFICE FULL FORM POSTMASTER LEVEL
GPO GENERAL POST OFFICE SENIOR POSTMASTER
HO HEAD OFFICE
PB PHILATELIC BUREAU DIRECTOR or INCHARGE
SO SUB OFFICE SUB POSTMASTER
BO BRANCH OFFICE BRANCH POSTMASTER

The below format is the proper way to write an address on the envelope when writing to a post office to collect a cancellation.

Sample Address

FORMAT EXAMPLE
______ Postmaster,
{Post Office and Rank},
{Name of the town/city},
{District (dist.)},
{State} - {Pincode}. 		Sub Postmaster,
Shaniwar Wada SO,
Pune,
Pune (dist.),
Maharashtra - 411030.
